ZooLights Death: Police Recover “Murder Weapon” in Great Cats area; Shift Focus to Staff Access

by Caitlin Chameleon | Washington, D.C.

Police investigating tonight’s fatal incident at ZooLights say they’ve recovered what appears to be the murder weapon: a strand of Christmas lights consistent with ZooLights spare inventory.

Investigators noted the lighting strand was not a personal item and is typically stored in staff-only areas for maintenance and emergency replacements.

With that discovery, detectives say they are narrowing interviews to zoo staff and anyone with restricted access as they work to confirm a timeline and review witness statements.
